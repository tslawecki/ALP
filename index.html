Additional Buoy Information

This buoy is located offshore of Cleveland near the water intake crib. Funding for the station is provided by the City of Cleveland, funds are managed by the Great Lakes Observing System, and the station is owned and maintained by LimnoTech. The station monitors atmospheric conditions, waves, water temperature, surface water quality, and bottom water quality including dissolved oxygen.
