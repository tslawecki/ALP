    The Cleveland Intake Crib buoy (Station 45176) is located in Lake Erie offshore of Cleveland, Ohio near the city's water intake crib. The buoy is owned and maintained by LimnoTech, which receives funding from the City of Cleveland managed by the Great Lakes Observing System. It monitors atmospheric conditions, waves, water temperature, surface water quality, and bottom water quality including dissolved oxygen.
